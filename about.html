About Bridge

Make Room.

Bridge is about creating space -- space for people to store, create, gather, and grow. What started as a self-storage facility has evolved into a creative community hub in Richmond, California.

We believe that when you make room in your life, extraordinary things happen. Our facility brings together artists, entrepreneurs, families, and neighbors under one roof -- connected by murals, music, and a shared sense of possibility.

What We Offer

Storage

Climate-controlled units from 5x5 to 10x30, each with a unique hand-painted mural door. Safe, secure, and accessible daily.

Event Spaces

A versatile dance floor, outdoor courtyard, and gallery space for parties, workshops, performances, and community gatherings.

Commercial Kitchen

A fully equipped commercial kitchen available 24/7 for food entrepreneurs, caterers, and culinary creatives.

Art Studios

Dedicated studio spaces for artists-in-residence, with natural light, flexible layouts, and a supportive creative community.

Woodshop

A shared woodshop with professional tools, mentoring, and classes for makers of all skill levels.

Gallery

A rotating gallery showcasing local artists. The space doubles as an event venue for openings, talks, and private events.

Facility Highlights

Murals on Every Door

Over 300 hand-painted mural doors by local artists make Bridge one of the most colorful storage facilities anywhere.

Solar-Powered

Our facility runs on solar energy, reducing our environmental footprint and keeping operating costs low.

Free Electric Truck

Tenants get complimentary access to our branded electric truck for move-ins, move-outs, and errands around town.

Gated & Monitored

Secure gated access with 24/7 camera surveillance throughout the facility for your peace of mind.
