Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly through our website, including:

  • Contact information submitted through forms, such as your name, email address, phone number, and message content.
  • Usage data collected automatically, including pages visited, browser type, and IP address.
  • Cookies used for session management and essential site functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Process rental applications and service requests
  • Send service-related communications
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only with:

  • Payment processor (Stripe) for transactions processed through our tenant portal
  • Email and SMS service providers to deliver service-related communications
  • Legal authorities as required by law

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data is transmitted using SSL encryption. Payment processing is handled securely through our tenant portal and is never stored on this website.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality, such as maintaining your session and remembering your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the information provided below.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to our tenant portal and payment processor. These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
